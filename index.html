Crânes Ado: a little zine for print and tablets about average folk who make and play their own games. Video games, board games, physical games, mind games. Informal and abstract, Crânes Ado itself is a game of the artistic expression of sets of rules and the experience of play.

CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS:
Crânes Ado, Volume 1

Crânes Ado, a quarterly zine on the art of game making, is seeking submissions for its first issue coming in summer 20XX.

For our first issue, we are seeking lists of rules, art / photography, photojournalism, and essays / fiction / poetry. Submissions should be focused on the people who make games, the people who play them, or the art or rules or experience of personally created games.

Crânes Ado is an all volunteer effort and currently pays $.02/word and one printed copy.

Please send pitches or full-draft submissions to info@cranesado.com. We accept pitches and submissions on a rolling basis, but priority for Volume 1 will be given to those received by June 1, 20XX.
